How to Play

Guess the hidden 5-letter word in 6 attempts.

  • Type a 5-letter word using your keyboard or the on-screen keyboard.
  • Press Enter to submit your guess.
  • Press Backspace or Delete to remove a letter.
  • After each guess, the colors will show how close your guess was.

A The letter is correct and in the correct place.

B The letter is in the word, but in the wrong place.

C The letter is not in the word.
